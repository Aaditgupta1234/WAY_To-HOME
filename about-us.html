About Way To Home

Connecting travelers with the perfect accommodations since 2010

Our Mission

Way To Home exists to make it easier for everyone to experience the world. By investing in technology that takes the friction out of travel, we seamlessly connect millions of travelers to memorable experiences, a variety of transportation options, and incredible places to stay â€“ from homes to hotels, and much more.

As one of the world's largest travel marketplaces, we empower partners around the world to grow their businesses and make it easy for people everywhere to explore the world where and how they want to.

Our Story

Way To Home was founded in 2010 with a vision to revolutionize the way people book accommodations online. What began as a small startup with just 5 employees has grown into a global company with offices in over 70 countries worldwide.

Our journey started when our founders experienced the challenges of finding suitable accommodations during their travels. They believed there had to be a better way to connect travelers with property owners, making the booking process seamless and transparent.

Today, we've helped millions of travelers find their perfect stay, and we continue to innovate and expand our services to meet the evolving needs of modern travelers.

Sustainable Travel

At Way To Home, we recognize our responsibility to the environment and the communities we serve. We're committed to promoting sustainable travel practices and reducing our environmental footprint.

Our sustainability initiatives include:

  • Carbon offset programs for our operations
  • Highlighting eco-friendly accommodations
  • Partnering with local communities to promote sustainable tourism
  • Reducing waste in our offices worldwide
  • Supporting conservation efforts in popular tourist destinations

By making thoughtful choices, we believe travel can be a force for good in the world.
