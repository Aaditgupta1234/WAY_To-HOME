Cancellation Options

Understanding our flexible booking policies

About Our Cancellation Policies

At Way To Home, we understand that plans can change. That's why we offer various cancellation options to provide you with flexibility and peace of mind when booking your stay.

Important Notice

Cancellation policies may vary depending on the property and the type of rate you book. Always check the specific conditions before making your reservation.

Our Cancellation Policy Types

Free Cancellation

Fully Flexible

Maximum flexibility for your peace of mind

  • Cancel up to 24 hours before check-in
  • Receive a full refund
  • No questions asked
  • Best for uncertain travel plans
Partial Refund

Semi-Flexible

Balance between flexibility and better rates

  • Cancel up to 3 days before check-in
  • Receive a partial refund (typically 50-75%)
  • First night may be non-refundable
  • Good for planned trips with some flexibility
Non-Refundable

Non-Refundable

Best rates for confident travelers

  • No cancellation or changes allowed
  • Lowest possible rates
  • Full payment required at booking
  • Ideal for sure travel plans

How to Cancel Your Booking

Canceling your booking is simple and can be done in a few easy steps:

  1. Sign in to your Way To Home account
  2. Go to "My Bookings"
  3. Select the booking you want to cancel
  4. Click on "Cancel Booking"
  5. Confirm your cancellation

Processing Time

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days, depending on your payment method and financial institution.

Special Circumstances

We understand that extraordinary situations may require special consideration. Contact our customer service team if you need to cancel due to:

  • Natural disasters or severe weather conditions
  • Travel restrictions or government advisories
  • Medical emergencies (documentation may be required)
  • Other extenuating circumstances
